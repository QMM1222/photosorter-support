隐私政策

本隐私政策描述了PhotoSorter应用如何收集、使用和保护您的个人信息。我们高度重视您的隐私，并致力于保护您的个人数据。请仔细阅读本政策，了解我们的数据处理实践。

使用PhotoSorter应用即表示您同意本隐私政策中描述的数据处理活动。

信息收集

我们收集的信息

PhotoSorter仅收集应用功能所必需的最少信息：

  • 照片库访问：我们请求访问您设备上的照片库，以便您可以整理和管理照片。
  • 应用使用数据：我们可能收集有关您如何使用应用的基本匿名数据，例如功能使用频率和应用崩溃报告。
  • 设备信息：基本设备信息，如操作系统版本和设备型号，用于优化应用性能。

我们不收集的信息

PhotoSorter不会收集以下信息：

  • 您的照片内容或元数据不会上传到我们的服务器
  • 位置数据
  • 联系人信息
  • 浏览历史
  • 个人身份信息

信息使用

我们使用收集的信息：

  • 提供、维护和改进PhotoSorter应用
  • 响应您的请求和客户支持需求
  • 分析应用性能和用户体验
  • 检测、预防和解决技术问题

数据存储和处理

本地处理：PhotoSorter的主要照片处理全部在您的设备上本地进行。您的照片不会上传到我们的服务器，确保您的照片内容完全隐私。

数据存储：应用创建的所有相簿和整理数据都存储在您的设备本地。如果您选择在多设备间同步数据（仅限高级版用户），我们使用苹果的iCloud服务安全传输数据。

信息共享

我们不会出售、交易或出租您的个人信息给第三方。我们可能在以下情况下共享信息：

  • 服务提供商：我们可能与帮助我们提供服务的受信任第三方共享信息（如应用分析服务提供商）。
  • 法律要求：如果法律要求，或为保护我们的权利，我们可能会披露您的信息。

您的权利

您对自己的数据拥有以下权利：

  • 随时取消照片库访问权限
  • 要求我们删除您可能提供给我们的任何个人数据

数据安全

我们采取合理的安全措施保护您的个人信息免受未经授权的访问、更改、披露或销毁。所有照片处理在本地设备上进行，最大限度地减少数据泄露风险。

儿童隐私

PhotoSorter不面向13岁以下儿童。我们不会故意收集13岁以下儿童的个人信息。如果您是父母或监护人，发现您的孩子向我们提供了个人信息，请联系我们，我们将从我们的记录中删除这些信息。

政策变更

我们可能会不时更新我们的隐私政策。我们将通过在应用中发布新的隐私政策来通知您任何更改。建议您定期查看本政策以了解任何变更。

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how PhotoSorter app collects, uses, and protects your personal information. We highly value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. Please read this policy carefully to understand our data handling practices.

By using the PhotoSorter app, you agree to the data processing activities described in this Privacy Policy.

Information Collection

Information We Collect

PhotoSorter only collects the minimum information necessary for app functionality:

  • Photo Library Access: We request access to the photo library on your device so you can organize and manage your photos.
  • App Usage Data: We may collect basic anonymous data about how you use the app, such as feature usage frequency and app crash reports.
  • Device Information: Basic device information such as operating system version and device model for optimizing app performance.

Information We Don't Collect

PhotoSorter does not collect the following information:

  • Your photo content or metadata is not uploaded to our servers
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Browsing history
  • Personal identification information

Use of Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the PhotoSorter app
  • Respond to your requests and customer support needs
  • Analyze app performance and user experience
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues

Data Storage and Processing

Local Processing: PhotoSorter's primary photo processing is done entirely locally on your device. Your photos are not uploaded to our servers, ensuring complete privacy of your photo content.

Data Storage: All albums and organization data created by the app are stored locally on your device. If you choose to sync data across multiple devices (premium users only), we use Apple's iCloud service to securely transfer data.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third parties who assist us in providing our services (such as app analytics service providers).
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights, we may disclose your information.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Revoke photo library access permission at any time
  • Request deletion of any personal data you may have provided to us

Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All photo processing is done locally on your device, minimizing the risk of data breaches.

Children's Privacy

PhotoSorter is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you learn that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will remove that information from our records.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app. You are advised to review this Policy periodically for any changes.
